Nowadays, people face a variety of diseases due to their living habits and changing environmental conditions. It could have happened before you or anyone near you needed urgent assistance from the doctor, but you couldn't locate anyone. It is beneficial to get quick and adequate medical facilities for patients by developing a model that can predict diseases based on user symptoms. Many lives can be saved by the correct review of data and accurately predicting diseases from symptoms. But it becomes too difficult for a doctor to predict accurately based on symptoms. The most difficult task is the accurate prediction of disease. As generally said, "Prevention is better than cure," disease prediction and infectious outbreak will contribute to early prevention of a disease occurrence. A significant amount of data growth per year is seen in the healthcare industry. The accurate analysis of medical data will extract information to anticipate diseases that will happen to a patient in the future by using the medical data. With the aid of disease data, machine learning algorithms find hidden pattern knowledge in an enormous amount of medical data.
